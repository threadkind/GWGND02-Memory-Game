📖 How To Play

There are 16 cards (8 pairs) randomly arranged on the game board. The goal of the game is to flip over 2 cards at a time to try to find the matching pairs.

When you find a matching pair, the cards in the pair will stay face up so you know you have a match.

If the cards don't match then they will automatically flip back over and you can chose the next two cards you want to try to match

To win the game you need to find all 8 matching pairs of cards.

There are 3 decks of cards to choose from in the game: images, number and colors. The gameplay is the same for each deck, click the deck you want to use to start the game.
